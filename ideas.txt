MINIMALISM


The game will be a subversion of games. This way, it will start with some of the 
traditional elements of a game, but shed them away. Mainly, it will remove them
only if the player never used them. This reinforces their futility.


Basic game: - exploration zelda like.

elements:

	- enemy
	- pickups
		- sword
		- armor
		- health/powerup
